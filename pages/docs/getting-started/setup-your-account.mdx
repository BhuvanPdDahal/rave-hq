# Setup Your Account

Now let's help you setup your RaveHQ account and get started quickly. Follow the below steps to create a new account:

* Head over to the RaveHQ signin page [here](/signin).
* Enter your email address and create a secure password *or* signin with Google or GitHub.
* If you signed in using email and password, check your inbox, copy the token and enter it to the input box to verify your email.

Once logged in, you can personalize your profile by adding details like your name, a profile picture, and change your password directly from the settings page.
